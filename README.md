## Problem Statement
The factory has sales data containing customer, product, and regional information. The management requires detailed reports to understand sales distribution by product subcategory and state. The goal is to provide:

1. **Subcategory-wise Customer Sales Reports** – each subcategory in a separate Excel sheet.
2. **State-wise Subcategory Sales Reports** – each state in a separate Excel sheet.

These reports help the management identify sales trends across products, customers, and regions.
